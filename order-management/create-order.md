### Guide to Creating and Managing Orders in HIS System

&#x20;

**1. Navigate to Order Record** [0:00](https://loom.com/share/0a57de50b5e14556a557b2c85a8d9dfd?t=0)

![generated-image-at-00:00:00](https://loom.com/i/7424d3c8f1954268ad95ac4a934e90ee?workflows_screenshot=true)

* Access the HIS system.
* Locate and navigate to the order record section.
* You can either manage an existing order or create a new one.

&#x20;

**2. Pre-fill Order Details** [0:23](https://loom.com/share/0a57de50b5e14556a557b2c85a8d9dfd?t=23)

![generated-image-at-00:00:23](https://loom.com/i/c8c1551eddef492d8c0f3a957659fa1c?workflows_screenshot=true)

* The system will automatically pre-fill:
  * Doctor's information.
  * Dietitian's information (choose the pre-consultation dietitian).
  * Health secretary's information.

&#x20;

**3. Connect to SOAP Notes** [0:35](https://loom.com/share/0a57de50b5e14556a557b2c85a8d9dfd?t=35)

![generated-image-at-00:00:35](https://loom.com/i/d49db1ebd7074479ab23a18a7b81ab15?workflows_screenshot=true)

* Optionally connect to the SOAP notes created by the doctor.

&#x20;

**4. Add Additional Products and Examinations** [0:52](https://loom.com/share/0a57de50b5e14556a557b2c85a8d9dfd?t=52)

![generated-image-at-00:00:52](https://loom.com/i/55d9c528ac99400e99dab5c057942637?workflows_screenshot=true)

* If needed, add other products (e.g., M-body).
* You can also add examinations if the client requests more.

&#x20;

**5. Preview Order** [1:21](https://loom.com/share/0a57de50b5e14556a557b2c85a8d9dfd?t=81)

![generated-image-at-00:01:21](https://loom.com/i/c30eb3bc43b34884951b7c4d438ea9a8?workflows_screenshot=true)

* Click on 'Preview Order' to review all products added to the shopping list for the client.

&#x20;

**6. Confirm Client's Willingness to Pay** [1:36](https://loom.com/share/0a57de50b5e14556a557b2c85a8d9dfd?t=96)

![generated-image-at-00:01:36](https://loom.com/i/0da5dc7f09044626a951c31a20204c54?workflows_screenshot=true)

* Ensure that all items in the order are acceptable to the client and that they are willing to pay for them.

&#x20;

**7. Proceed to Checkout** [1:45](https://loom.com/share/0a57de50b5e14556a557b2c85a8d9dfd?t=105)

![generated-image-at-00:01:45](https://loom.com/i/a06dbfb7f36942db91e540c8c1eced26?workflows_screenshot=true)

* Click on 'Next Step' to proceed to checkout.

&#x20;

**8. Add Transaction Details** [1:57](https://loom.com/share/0a57de50b5e14556a557b2c85a8d9dfd?t=117)

![generated-image-at-00:01:57](https://loom.com/i/3b7cb0fb0c6b49c99394f27ee3353f29?workflows_screenshot=true)

* Choose 'Payment' (not 'Refund').
* Select the payment method:
  * Cash
  * Credit Card
* For this example, we will use cash.

&#x20;

**9. Enter Payment Amount** [2:07](https://loom.com/share/0a57de50b5e14556a557b2c85a8d9dfd?t=127)

![generated-image-at-00:02:07](https://loom.com/i/46ab03ed68dc420a8989c76910526db5?workflows_screenshot=true)

* Input the amount the client will pay in cash.

&#x20;

**10. Confirm Payment** [2:25](https://loom.com/share/0a57de50b5e14556a557b2c85a8d9dfd?t=145)

![generated-image-at-00:02:25](https://loom.com/i/5354d2e2191c4baabfd595282cbbe2ea?workflows_screenshot=true)

* Click 'Confirm' to finalize the payment.
* Alternatively, you can save the expected transaction for later.

&#x20;

**11. Return to Order List** [2:50](https://loom.com/share/0a57de50b5e14556a557b2c85a8d9dfd?t=170)

![generated-image-at-00:02:50](https://loom.com/i/63441e2bdf74495e83d3a6cc21970ff5?workflows_screenshot=true)

* After saving or confirming the payment, return to the order list to check the status of the order.

&#x20;

**12. Check Order Status** [3:07](https://loom.com/share/0a57de50b5e14556a557b2c85a8d9dfd?t=187)

![generated-image-at-00:03:07](https://loom.com/i/c42b0c52fb7b4ee1be3dc147ab3dc5f5?workflows_screenshot=true)

* Verify that the order is ready for checkout.

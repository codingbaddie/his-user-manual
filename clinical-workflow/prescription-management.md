**Step 2: Modify Prescription Selection** [0:17](https://loom.com/share/fcc90f1440f3480cab9c6ac3f0df7391?t=17)

![generated-image-at-00:00:17](https://loom.com/i/46271432a158487bb6d7d38c2ae46027?workflows_screenshot=true)

* If needed, uncheck any medicines you do not want to include.
* Confirm your selection after making changes.

&#x20;

**Step 3: Use Historical Prescriptions** [0:32](https://loom.com/share/fcc90f1440f3480cab9c6ac3f0df7391?t=32)

![generated-image-at-00:00:32](https://loom.com/i/04d08534aa33434880fa43d624d76ee8?workflows_screenshot=true)

* To use a historical prescription, click on the historical option.
* Select one or multiple historical prescriptions as needed.
* Confirm your selection.

&#x20;

**Step 4: Add Examinations** [0:45](https://loom.com/share/fcc90f1440f3480cab9c6ac3f0df7391?t=45)

![generated-image-at-00:00:45](https://loom.com/i/ef770aec95fe4395bc021a1a33613bbd?workflows_screenshot=true)

* Click on the inspection option to add examinations for your patient.
* Choose the necessary examinations and confirm your selection.

&#x20;

**Step 5: Complete and Send** [1:25](https://loom.com/share/fcc90f1440f3480cab9c6ac3f0df7391?t=85)

* Make any necessary changes to ensure all fields are filled correctly.
* Click 'complete' to finalize the update.
